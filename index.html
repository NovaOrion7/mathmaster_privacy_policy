At Math Master ("we", "our", or "application"), we place the highest value on your privacy. This policy transparently explains how your information is handled.

1. Data Collection Policy

Math Master is an offline-first application designed with privacy in mind:

  • No personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.) is collected.
  • No account creation is required to use the app.
  • Your progress (15 levels, 16 badges earned, avatar selections, and statistics) is stored exclusively locally on your device.

2. 15-Level Progression & Data

Your XP points and level milestones (up to the 15th 'Godlike' level) are kept within your device's secure application storage. This data is never shared with any server or transmitted externally.

3. Third-Party Services & Ads

Our application does not include third-party analytics tools or advertising networks that track user behavior. Your learning journey remains entirely private.

4. Children's Privacy

Designed for math lovers of all ages, we do not collect data from children, ensuring full compliance with COPPA and international privacy standards.

5. Security

Your data is protected by the inherent security layers of your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all locally stored progress data.
